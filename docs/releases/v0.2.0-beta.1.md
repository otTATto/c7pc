## ⏱️ What is c7pc?

c7pc is a desktop port of Chal7777 — a timing game where you try to stop the timer at exactly 7.777 seconds. The countdown display fades out along the way, so you have to trust your inner clock.

## ✨ New Features

- **Daily ranking** 🏆 — every result is now saved locally (SQLite, on your device only), and a new ranking screen after the result shows today's top 10 plus your current rank
- Rows outside the top 10 are folded with vertical ellipses (⋮), and your own row is highlighted when you make the top 10
- Land in the top 3 and your rank glows gold / silver / bronze with a confetti celebration
- The result screen button now leads to the ranking screen (「今日のランキングを見に行く」)
- Minimum window size is now 800 x 900 (width x height)

> 🍎 **macOS users**:

>   ```sh

>   ```
